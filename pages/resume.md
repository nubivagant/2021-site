# Kurtis Johnson

I'm a strategic organisational change and people consultant with over eight years experience leading complex transformation programmes across government, health and housing. I work across the full spectrum of people and organisational change — from culture and capability to operating model design — leading multidisciplinary teams and building the conditions for lasting, meaningful change.

I'm drawn to problems where the human system is as consequential as the technical one. My approach combines rigorous organisation design practice with user-centred thinking, always grounded in outcomes that matter to the people and communities being served.

<small>organisation design | culture & change | capability & coaching | transformation</small>

## Professional experience

Leading multidisciplinary consulting engagements across people and organisational change, operating model design, and capability development — primarily in government, health and housing sectors.

**Culture & organisational change:** Leading cultural and behavioural change programmes at organisational scale, working with senior leadership teams to shift ways of working, embed new practices, and sustain change beyond project delivery. Contributed to business development and proposal writing throughout, shaping client opportunities and drawing together multi-disciplinary expertise into compelling bids.

**Stakeholder and client management:** Building trusted relationships with senior stakeholders including SLT, policy leads, elected members and programme directors, across organisations with varying levels of change maturity. Recognised for translating complex organisational concepts clearly for non-technical audiences and bridging across professional and political cultures.
